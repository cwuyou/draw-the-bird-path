How to Play

Getting started with Draw the Bird Path is wonderfully simple, yet mastering it provides endless satisfaction. Using your mouse on desktop or finger on mobile devices, draw paths directly on the screen to guide birds from their starting positions to their target destinations. The birds will automatically follow the routes you create, so precision and planning are key to success.

As you progress through the levels, you'll encounter various obstacles like walls, moving platforms, and environmental hazards that require creative solutions. Some levels feature multiple birds that need to reach different destinations, adding layers of complexity to your path-drawing strategy. The game rewards both efficiency and creativity, with bonus points available for elegant solutions.

Tips and Strategies

Success in Draw the Bird Path comes from understanding bird behavior and environmental physics. Birds follow your drawn paths faithfully, but they're also affected by gravity and momentum. Consider drawing gentle curves rather than sharp angles to maintain smooth movement, and always account for the birds' natural flight patterns when planning your routes.

Take time to study each level before drawing your first path. Identify all destinations, obstacles, and moving elements. Don't be afraid to restart a level if your initial approach isn't working; experimentation is part of the fun!
